VENDOR COMPLIANCE GUIDELINES

APPROVED VENDORS
- All purchases must be from pre-approved vendor list
- New vendors require compliance verification
- Vendor performance is monitored quarterly

QUALITY STANDARDS
- All materials must meet ISO 9001 standards
- Certificates of compliance required for critical items
- Random quality audits may be conducted

PRICING AGREEMENTS
- Contract prices are fixed for the agreement period
- Volume discounts apply as per contract terms
- Emergency purchases may have premium pricing

DELIVERY REQUIREMENTS
- Standard delivery window is 5-10 business days
- Express delivery available at additional cost
- Delivery confirmation required for all orders

INVOICE PROCESSING
- Invoices must be submitted within 30 days of delivery
- Electronic invoicing preferred
- All invoices require proper documentation
