PROCUREMENT POLICY GUIDELINES

1. PRICE VARIATIONS
- Price variations up to 5% from the original PO are acceptable for standard items
- Price variations above 5% require manager approval
- Price variations above 10% require director approval

2. QUANTITY DISCREPANCIES
- Quantity variations up to 2% are acceptable for bulk orders
- Over-delivery of up to 5% is acceptable if storage permits
- Under-delivery requires vendor justification

3. ITEM SUBSTITUTIONS
- Equivalent items from approved vendors are acceptable
- Substitutions must maintain same quality standards
- Brand substitutions require prior approval

4. DELIVERY TERMS
- Early delivery is acceptable if storage permits
- Late delivery beyond agreed terms requires penalty assessment
- Partial deliveries are acceptable for large orders

5. INVOICE REQUIREMENTS
- All invoices must match PO line items
- Tax calculations must be accurate
- Payment terms must match PO terms
